Report a Kill



Kills MUST be reported within three days of the kill!

This form is for active zombies to report a kill. To report a kill, you MUST have your player code, the player code of the human you have devoured, and the approximate time of death. The human will be converted into a zombie, and the zombie's feed timer will be reset. An email will be sent to both you and the human you have killed.
DISHONEST REPORTING OF KILLS MAY RESULT IN EXPULSION FROM THE GAME. BE HONEST, PEOPLE.
