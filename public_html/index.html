What is HVZ? (A concise description)

Humans Vs. Zombies is an extreme, campus-wide game of tag. There are two teams: The Humans and the Zombies. The zombies' goal is to eat and infect all the humans, increasing their ranks. The humans' goal is to stay alive until all the zombies die of starvation. Zombies can catch humans by tagging them; humans can stun zombies by shooting them with Nerf guns or swords. The game goes on for at least a week, making class changes and trips to Slate a hazard for unprepared humans! Think you're a survivor? Register now, under "Register" above!
